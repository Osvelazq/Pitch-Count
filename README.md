A simple, intuitive, and mobile-optimized web application for tracking baseball pitching stats (strikes, balls, strikeouts, and walks).
​Features
​Real-time Tracking: Easily log strikes and balls with large, touch-friendly buttons designed for field use.
​Automated Logic: Automatically calculates strikeouts and walks based on standard baseball rules.
​Local Persistence: Data is automatically saved to your device's browser using localStorage. Your stats remain intact even if you refresh or close the app.
​Offline Ready: Once loaded, the app functions entirely offline.
​Visual Analytics: Includes an integrated bar chart to visualize your strikeout-to-walk ratio instantly.
